Three steps, every time

The method doesn't change. The gear just makes it repeatable.

  1. 01

    Bloom

    Pour twice the coffee's weight in water. Wait thirty seconds while it degasses. Skip this and the rest of the pour extracts unevenly.

  2. 02

    Pour

    Add the remaining water in slow, steady circles. A gooseneck kettle keeps the stream thin enough to control where it lands.

  3. 03

    Rest

    Let the last of the water drain fully before you lift the dripper. Total brew time: three to four minutes, start to finish.
